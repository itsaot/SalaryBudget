How the Payslip Amounts are Calculated

UIF Calculation

The Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF) deduction is calculated at 1% of your monthly income. This is deducted to provide financial assistance if you become unemployed.

Pension Fund Calculation

The pension fund deduction is 7% of your monthly income, which goes toward your retirement fund.

Income Tax Calculation

Income tax is calculated based on the South African tax brackets for the current tax year. Below is a breakdown of how tax is applied based on your annual income:

  • Annual income up to R237,100: 18% tax
  • R237,101 to R370,500: 26% tax on the amount exceeding R237,100
  • R370,501 to R512,800: 31% tax on the amount exceeding R370,500
  • R512,801 to R673,000: 36% tax on the amount exceeding R512,800
  • R673,001 to R857,900: 39% tax on the amount exceeding R673,000
  • R857,901 to R1,817,000: 41% tax on the amount exceeding R857,900
  • Above R1,817,000: 45% tax on the amount exceeding R1,817,000
