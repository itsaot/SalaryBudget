How the Amounts are Calculated

The payslip calculations include monthly deductions for UIF (Unemployment Insurance Fund), Pension Fund, and Income Tax. Here's a summary of how each is calculated:

  • UIF (1%): 1% of your monthly income.
  • Pension Fund (7%): 7% of your monthly income.
  • Income Tax: Calculated based on South Africa's tax brackets for the year.
